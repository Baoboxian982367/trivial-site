Inspiration

    Hello every one! My name is Agnes Bao.In my opinion, as one necessity in life, art also needs to be integrated into life. Actually, all of them are very special endowed with their respective unique meanings. And in my design works, there are several series, such as Starry-sky Series, Abstract-geometry Series and Animal Series. In my design works of "Starry-sky Series", I added multiple elements, like star and moon, as well as adopted the unique bright blue as the combination with the starry sky, better presenting a particularly artistic work. And I think the person wearing it would achieve gentle appearance.

    Abstract Geometry Series can be said a relatively high-end series among them. You can choose suit or white shirt when wearing it. By combining simple and generous clothing with the cute earrings full of geometric elements, I believe you would be eye-catching. And these exquisite ornaments would add you more high-end senses and connotations. In addition, Animal Series is believed to be the very cute series, such as kitten ears and puppy collars, fit for multiple casual occasions.
